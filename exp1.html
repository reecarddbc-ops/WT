Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Al Nassr and the Portugal national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time, Ronaldo has won numerous trophies including Champions League titles, domestic league titles, and international honors.
